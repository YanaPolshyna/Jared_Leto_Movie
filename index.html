Best of Jared Leto

Today it is even difficult to say whether Jared Leto is more of a musician or an actor. It seems that he acts in films more than he sings at Thirty Seconds to Mars concerts. We recall the 3 most interesting roles of Leto to date.

"Suicide Squad"

Summary

"Suicide Squad" - this is how they dubbed a group of dangerous criminals and outcasts who must complete a dangerous task. They have life sentences in the most terrible dungeons, they have nothing to lose. A deadly mission with a collar around their neck that explodes as soon as they try to escape is the only option to leave their cells.

"American Psycho"

Summary

American Psycho - during the day, he is no different from others, and in the crowd you will not pay attention to him. But at night, this well-bred citizen turns into a sophisticated killer, terrorizing the sleeping city. The modern savage, who despises the laws of mankind, feeds only on the sizzling flame of his hatred, which grows with each new crime. An avalanche of horror is approaching a critical point. Soon there will come a time when it will no longer be possible to stop it.

"Blade Runner"

Summary

"Blade Runner" - in the not-too-distant future, the world is populated by humans and replicants, made to do the hardest jobs. Police Officer Kay's job is to keep the replicants under control in the face of mounting tension. He accidentally becomes the owner of secret information that threatens the existence of all mankind. Wanting to find a clue, Kay decides to track down Rick Deckard, a former LAPD Special Forces officer who disappeared without a trace many years ago.
